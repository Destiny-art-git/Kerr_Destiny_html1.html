Paw-spiration

My tribute goes out to those that dedicate their life to help animals in need. I believe animals can help fill a hole in your heart, help with depression and anxiety and bring joy and laughter to your life. I will always adopt, not shop. I hope that others can see how much of a difference we can make by giving dogs and cats a second chance and a loving home. Below are 2 organizations and 1 club located in Utah dedicated to making a difference.

Nuzzles and Co

Located in Summit County Utah and started by a group of volunteers, Nuzzles and Co have found homes for over 22,000 pets. In their 16,000 square foot Resuce Ranch they have a large community cat room and large outdoor enrichment runs for dogs to exercise and play. My family has become very fond of Nuzzles and Co because we adopted our dog Boba there a few months ago. He has filled out hearts with joy. If you would like to adopt an animal or donate to them click the link to their website below.

Best Friends Animal Society

Starting in the 1990's, Best Friends has been a leader in making Utah a no-kill state. In 2000, they began a program called No More Homeless Pets (now called Best Friends NKUT). Their initiative was to bring together resuce programs, shelters, goverment and businesses to help save animals by making adopting more accessible. The program has reduced the number of animals killed each year. Before the program began 46,000 animals were killed in shelters in Utah every year. By 2018 that number had been reduced to under 3,500. Learn More about their amazing work by visiting their website.
